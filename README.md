## Part1: Imaging Technique Inspiration

### Cinematic Atmospheric Rendering in Beeple’s Digital Art
One of the biggest inspiration is from the artists Beeple, whose digital artworks use cinematic atmospheric rendering to create immersive futuristic environments. I am mainly inspired by the use of glow, layered lighting, haze, and colour gradients in his work. These visual elements create a strong sense of mood and spatial depth, making the environments feel more cinematic and emotionally engaging. I plan to apply similar atmospheric effects in the final project to explore how layered colour can influence visual storytelling. This technique is useful because it can transform simple digital scenes into more immersive and expressive experiences.
> Key Observation: Beeple uses atmospheric rendering to contrust immersive digital environments that evoke futuristic and cinematic moods.
