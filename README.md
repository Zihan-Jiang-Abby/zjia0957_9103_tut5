## Part1: Imaging Technique Inspiration
### Cinematic Atmospheric Rendering in Beeple’s Digital Art
One of my greatest source of inspiration is the globally acclaimed digital artist Beeple, whose artworks are renowned for using cinematic atmospheric rendering to create immersive futuristic environments. What draws me most is his masterful use of glow, layered lighting, haze, and colour gradients ---- elements that establish a powerful sense of mood and spatial depth. I find it compelling to translate these atmospheric techniques into my final project as a means of storytelling, transforming simple digital scenes into something far more immersive and emotionally expressive.
> Key Observation: Beeple uses atmospheric rendering to contrust immersive digital environments that evoke futuristic and cinematic moods.
